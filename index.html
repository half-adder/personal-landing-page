Sean Johnsen

PhD Candidate · McKay Lab · University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill

I am a sixth-year PhD candidate in the McKay Lab at UNC Chapel Hill, where I study developmental epigenetics in Drosophila with a particular focus on Polycomb regulation during embryogenesis.

Prior to UNC, I received my B.S. in Biology and Computer Science at Northeastern University, where I worked in the Apfeld Lab studying oxidative stress and aging in C. elegans.
